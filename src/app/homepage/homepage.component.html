Your all-in-one calculator

This calculator is designed to make sure you stay as fantastic as you are.

How it works?

This calculator requires some information to be filled in order to provide the ideal weight for the person, with the characterstics provided. After submitting the information needed, the calculator will calculate the ideal weight and compare it with the person's weight. The calculator will also provides a visual graph that pictures the weight of the person and a graph that analyzes the person's weight and the over-weight amount.
